GAUSS: Graph-Aligned Uncertainty for Long-Form LLM Generation
Sep 2025
  • Modeled each generated paragraph as a semantic graph of atomic facts and inter-fact relations, and defined uncertainty as the expected alignment cost between an anchor graph and alternative generations via an optimal-transport-based graph distance.
  • Showed that GAUSS yields uncertainty scores that better track factual consistency and structural coherence than probability- and calibration-based baselines on long-form clinical, legal, and encyclopedic benchmarks.
